Mashed Potato

Description

A lazy mashed potato recipe that is sure to make you want to go out and get something real to eat. Creamy to a level of mediocrity and as boring as its stagnant color. Warning: Don't eat if you are allergic to potatoes.

Ingredients

  • 3 potatoes (golden or russet)
  • water
  • salt, pepper
  • 1 block of butter

Steps

  1. Throw potatoes into a pot
  2. Add water until potatoes are covered
  3. Sprinkle salt into the water
  4. Boil until potatoes are mushy (fork test)
  5. Take potatoes out and mash them in a bowl with a utensil of your choice
  6. Run potatoes through a sieve to filter out skin
  7. Cube the block of butter and add all cubes in
  8. Mash the potato and butter cubes, sprinkling in salt and pepper to your liking
  9. Eat
