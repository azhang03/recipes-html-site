Boba Milk Tea

Description

A somewhat violent boba milk tea recipe that is sure to make you never want to drink boba again. This recipe requires the cook to own or have access to a dwarf hamster or Great Horned Toad farm. It will be the most ambiguous milk tea you've ever tasted.

Ingredients

  • 10 dwarf hamsters/Great Horned Toads
  • milk
  • tea

Steps

  1. Mix half parts milk and tea into a cup
  2. Extract the beady eyes from your hamsters/toads
  3. Deposite the eyes into the milk tea
  4. Eat
