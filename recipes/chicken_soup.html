Chicken Soup

Description

A lazy chicken soup recipe that you can toss all of your sorrows and miscellaneous food scraps into. It should deliver a little comfort in every spoonful. Perfect for any day, and it's homemade without hassle. Sounds like a good deal, no?

Ingredients

  • 1 chicken
  • water
  • some carrots
  • some celery
  • some onion
  • salt, pepper, garlic powder
  • any other left over food scraps

Steps

  1. Throw chicken into a pot
  2. Add water until it looks like soup
  3. Toss carrots, celery, onion, and any other food scraps in
  4. Sprinkle in salt, pepper, and garlic powder to your liking
  5. Boil until chicken isn't suspiciously pink
  6. Eat
